Inner Demon

An Adventure in the Belly of the Beast.
一場在巨獸腹中的冒險。

Amidst the chaotic and tangled web of lines, an invisible beast lurks. It feels as if you're engulfed in its maw, surrounded by dark, intricate patterns, like the unconfrontable shadows deep within your soul. This adventure is not only a battle against your inner demons, but also a metaphor for self-discovery and redemption.
在無序與糾纏的線條之間，潛藏著一頭無形的巨獸。你彷彿被吞沒於牠的腹中，四周是幽暗而錯綜的紋理，如同心靈深處難以直面的陰影。這趟冒險，不僅是一場對抗心魔的旅程，更是一段自我探索與救贖的隱喻。

...

The work, with its free yet oppressive brushstrokes, symbolizes the chaos and struggle within the mind. Every line could be a way out or a trap; only by gazing bravely can one find a crack of light within the beast's depths.
作品以自由卻充滿壓迫感的筆觸，象徵心靈中混亂與掙扎的狀態。每一條線皆可能是出口，也可能是陷阱；唯有勇敢凝視，方能在巨獸的深處，找到光的裂縫。
