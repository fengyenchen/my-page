Daily Life in the Park

This is a series of works inspired by parks. Parks are the most everyday corners in the city, where different objects weave together to create fragments of order, rest, and life.
這是一組以「公園」為靈感的作品。公園是城市裡最貼近日常的角落，不同的物件在這裡交織出秩序、休憩與生活的片段。

Park chairs are always waiting to be occupied. Some come to rest, some to chat, and sometimes they're simply left there. In those moments, you can choose to stop and let yourself breathe.
公園的椅子總是等著人來坐。有人來小憩、有人閒聊，有時候也只是單純地放著。那一刻，你可以選擇停下來，讓自己慢慢呼吸。

Park traffic cones always stand quietly. They indicate the direction of movement and sometimes become playthings for children. In this space, every object has its own story.
公園的三角錐總是靜靜地矗立著。它們標示著行進的方向，有時候也成為小朋友們的遊戲道具。在這片空間裡，每一個物件都有自己的故事。
