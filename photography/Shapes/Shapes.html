Shapes

This series of photographs takes shapes as the theme, exploring their expressiveness and emotions in different scenes.
本系列攝影作品以形狀為主題，探索其在不同場景中的表現力與情感。

Rectangle

The rectangle is a regular and stable shape. The repeated pattern in the picture creates a sense of visual harmony.
矩形是個規則且穩固的形狀。圖中重複的圖案在視覺上創造出一種和諧感。

Triangle

The triangle has stability. The structure in the picture reminds me of a pyramid, presenting a symmetrical beauty, as if establishing a modern landmark in the city.
三角形具有穩定性。圖中的結構讓我聯想到金字塔，呈現出一種對稱的美，彷彿在城市中建立了一座現代化的地標。

Circle

The circle is soft and complete, without sharp edges or corners, giving people a sense of perfection and harmony. The picture shows a drainage hole. I converted the photo to black and white to add a sense of horror and mystery.
圓形柔和且完整，沒有尖銳的邊邊角角，給人帶來圓滿、和諧的感覺。圖中是一個排水孔，我把照片調成黑白的，多了些恐怖且神祕的感覺。
