Flow

This series of photographs uses motion blur as its theme. By capturing people and objects in motion, it presents the flow of time and visual tension, creating a unique atmosphere between clarity and blur.
這組攝影作品以運動模糊為主題，透過捕捉運動中的人物和物體，呈現出時間的流動和視覺的張力，在清晰與模糊之間營造出獨特的氛圍。

When light is freed from its source, it itself becomes a fluid subject. Through the swing of the lens, colors overlap and shuttle through the darkness, leaving behind a silky, ethereal trail that is pure and captivating.
當光擺脫了光源，它本身就成了流動的主體。透過鏡頭的擺動，色彩在黑暗中交疊、穿梭，留下了如絲綢般虛幻的軌跡，純粹而迷人。

The ripples on the lake surface showcase the beauty of flow amidst the interplay of light and shadow.
湖面上微波蕩漾，光影交錯中展現出流動的美感。

The flow of a city is a compression of speed and time. Long exposures converge car lights into an uninterrupted river of light, surging through the still nightscape, revealing the vibrant pulse of the city.
城市的流動，是速度與時間的壓縮。長時間曝光將車燈匯聚成不間斷的光河，在靜止的夜景中奔騰，展現了都市的生命脈動。
