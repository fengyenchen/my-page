Lines

This series of photographs takes lines as the theme, exploring their expressiveness and emotions in different scenes.
本系列攝影作品以線條為主題，探索其在不同場景中的表現力與情感。

Straight Line

The vertical lines give people a sense of solemnity. The picture is actually a wall with a shape. I am close to the wall, peeking through the gaps to see the world inside.
垂直線給人帶點莊嚴的感覺。圖中其實是一面有造型的牆，我貼近牆面，透過縫隙偷窺著裡面的世界。

Diagonal Line

The lines in the picture extend in one direction, which makes the picture neater and more orderly, and makes my eyes move unconsciously towards the intersection.
圖中的線條往一個方向延伸，這讓畫面更整齊且有秩序，使我的視線不自覺地往交會點移動。

The diagonal line adds a sense of movement and vitality to the static scene.
斜線的動感與活力，為靜態的場景注入了一絲生機。

Arc Line

The curved line adds a sense of softness and fluidity to the static scene.
弧線的柔和與流暢，為靜態的場景增添了一份動感。

The arc should bring a sense of softness, but in this composition, it turns into a diagonal line that divides the picture. It breaks the regularity of the horizontal line and makes my eyes unconsciously follow this boundary.
弧線本應帶來柔和感，但在這個構圖裡，它轉變為一道分割畫面的斜線。它打破了水平線的規律，讓我的視線不自覺地跟著這道邊界移動。

Curved Line

The curves give the picture a sense of flow while being neat, giving it a lively and non-rigid feel. The picture is made up of curves connected by obstacles, giving the impression of being able to peek behind something.
曲線讓畫面在整齊中又帶著一種流動感，有種活潑不死板的感覺。圖中是一個個障礙物連成的曲線，畫面有種躲在後面偷窺的感覺。

The curve formed by the leaves is placed on the straight line of the ditch, like a small river.
由樹葉們組成的曲線擺在水溝的直線上，像條小河。
