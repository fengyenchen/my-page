Motion blur

This series of photographs uses motion blur as its theme. By capturing people and objects in motion, it presents the flow of time and visual tension, creating a unique atmosphere between clarity and blur.
本系列攝影作品以動態模糊為題，透過捕捉移動中的人與物，呈現時間流動與視覺張力，營造介於清晰與模糊之間的獨特氛圍。

The pedestrian moves through the city, and the motion blur effect fills the scene with a sense of time flowing.
行人在城市中穿梭，動態模糊的效果讓畫面充滿了時間的流動感。

The vibrant colors of the rides merged into abstract light trails amidst the violent vibrations, perfectly capturing the pure energy and vitality.
遊樂設施鮮豔的色彩在劇烈震動中融合成抽象的光軌，完美記錄了那份純粹的能量與活力。

Through focused photography, the background is transformed into a Mercedes-Benz color block, highlighting the taxi's sense of speed. The portrait advertisement on the window is clearly visible in this flowing light, creating a moment of stillness within movement.
透過追焦攝影，將背景化為奔馳的色塊，凸顯了計程車的速度感。車窗上的人像廣告，在這片流光中反而清晰可見，形成動中有靜的瞬間。

The wind is invisible, but the lens allows you to see its shape. Every moment you step on the road, the world blurs into a poem about moving forward.
風是無形的，但鏡頭讓你看見了它的形狀。踩下的每一個瞬間，都把世界模糊成一首關於前進的詩。
