A glimpse of Daan Forest Park

This series of photos captures those casual moments in Daan Forest Park, showing the integration of nature and urban life.
這組照片捕捉了大安森林公園的那些隨意瞬間，展現了自然與城市生活的融合。

A cluster of hydrangeas in full bloom displays a rich gradation of blue to purple. A closer look reveals that some petals have wilted slightly, capturing the natural cycle of life—a quiet and authentic beauty revealed in its imperfections.
一叢盛開的繡球花，展現了由藍至紫的豐富層次。細看之下，部分花瓣已略顯枯萎，這恰好捕捉了生命的自然循環——在不完美中，反而透著一股靜謐而真實的美感。

Through the use of a shallow depth of field, the image gradually shifts from clarity to blur, suggesting the boundary between vision and perception. The cold, hard metal, arranged in a regular pattern, creates an atmosphere of both oppression and expansion, evoking the hidden order and norms of public spaces.
透過淺景深的處理，畫面逐漸由清晰走向模糊，暗示著視覺與感知的界線。冷硬的金屬材質在規律的排列下，展現出既壓抑又延展的氛圍，使人聯想到公共空間中隱藏的秩序與規範。

The playful straw bear installation art is a delightful addition to the park. The large bear gently embraces the small bear, and the simple material and intimate gesture add a warm and soothing atmosphere to the lush greenery.
充滿童趣的稻草熊裝置藝術，是公園裡討喜的風景。大熊溫柔地環抱著小熊，樸實的材質與親暱的姿態，為一片綠意盎然增添了許多溫馨與療癒的氣氛。

Visitors gather on the viewing platform, enjoying the scenery and relaxing. Their figures, along with the green trees along the shore, cast clear yet slightly swaying reflections on the calm water. Here, movement and stillness, reality and illusion intertwine, creating the most tranquil and typical scene of daily life in the park.
觀景平台上聚集了賞景、休憩的遊人，他們的身影與岸邊的綠樹，一同在平靜的水面上映出了清晰又略帶晃動的倒影。動與靜、真實與虛幻在此交織，是公園日常裡最寧靜又典型的畫面。

Through the vibrant foreground of flowers, the camera captures two tourists intently snapping photos with their phones. This photo captures the "recorded moment," an intriguing meta-image—in the same space, each person strives to cherish the beauty before them through their own perspective.
透過前景繽紛的花叢，鏡頭捕捉到兩位正專注用手機拍照的遊客。這張照片記錄了「記錄的瞬間」，一個有趣的後設畫面——在同一個空間裡，每個人都在用自己的視角，努力珍藏眼前的美好。

A unique mushroom-shaped pavilion stands quietly on the lawn, its roof dotted with stained glass windows. When sunlight filters through, it surely casts beautiful specks of light on the ground. It's more than just a sheltered place to rest; it's also a work of art imbued with playful charm and design.
一座造型獨特的蘑菇狀涼亭靜立在草地上，屋頂上點綴著彩色的玻璃圓窗。當陽光穿透時，想必會在地面灑下美麗的光斑。它不僅是供人歇息的遮蔽處，本身也是一件充滿童趣與設計感的藝術品。
