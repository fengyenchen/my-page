Park

This photography series focuses on parks, capturing the often-overlooked corners of daily life and showcasing the tranquility and beauty of urban green spaces.
本系列攝影作品以公園為主題，捕捉日常生活中常見卻容易被忽略的角落，展現都市綠地的寧靜與美感。

The traffic cone stands quietly in a corner of the park, adding a splash of color and reminding people of safety.
三角錐靜靜地佇立在公園一角，為日常景色增添一抹鮮明色彩，也提醒著人們安全的重要。

The bench by the court has witnessed countless games and moments of rest, serving as the perfect spot for sports and socializing.
球場邊的椅子，見證了無數場比賽與休憩時光，是運動與交流的最佳場所。

The building near the park blends into the cityscape, offering a unique contrast to the greenery.
公園附近的建築物與城市景觀融合，為綠意增添一份獨特的對比。

The spacious park bench offers visitors a place to rest and enjoy the embrace of greenery and sunlight.
寬敞的公園椅子，提供遊客歇息片刻，享受綠意與陽光的溫暖擁抱。

The deer statue quietly guards the park, symbolizing the harmonious coexistence of nature and life.
鹿銅像靜靜守護著公園，象徵著自然與生命的和諧共存。

This modern building stands close to the park, highlighting the harmony between architecture and nature.
這座現代建築鄰近公園，展現建築與自然的和諧共存。
